About TechClub

Founded in 2020, TechClub has grown into a vibrant community of passionate students dedicated to exploring and advancing technology.

Our Story

TechClub began as a small group of computer science students who shared a passion for technology and wanted to create a space where like-minded individuals could learn, collaborate, and grow together.

What started as weekly coding sessions has evolved into a comprehensive program featuring workshops, hackathons, guest speakers, and industry partnerships.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most active student organizations on campus, with over 200 members from diverse academic backgrounds united by their love for technology.

Our alumni have gone on to work at top tech companies, start their own ventures, and continue making meaningful contributions to the tech industry.
